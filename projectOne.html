D&AD

2023

As the creative industry evolves, institutions such as D&AD face greater scrutiny, both in terms of perceptions of elitism and the need to prove their value to teams, businesses and clients. With this in mind, we were tasked with creating a campaign to show that the D&AD Awards are for everyone.

Working as part of an integrated team from our offices in London and Sydney, we reached out to our creative networks, speaking to everyone from 3D designers to motion designers to writers to understand what D&AD means to them. We then created a campaign that celebrates everything that goes into making D&AD worthy work. It was my role to make multiple iterations of the iconic D&AD pencil to capture the idiosyncrasies of different crafts. I then brought these pencils to life, creating all the motion used across the campaign, awards and festival.
