Rolus

2024

ROLUS is a new-to-market alcohol alternative made with electrolytes and adaptogens to keep you hydrated and on-the-go. It’s made for those who are Never Still. So the brand needed to reflect the energy and the dynamism that the drink can give people—without the usual nonsense the category comes loaded with.

Motion is at the heart for the brand. Movement is direct, not decorative. The product itself and its ingredients are given a rolling movement. Same for the Never Still tagline. And footage is untreated and does away with graphic elements like transitions to feel immediate and authentic.
