Sydney Festival

2021-2023

The Sydney Festival is a major arts festival held every January, featuring a diverse array of performances and exhibitions from both Australian and international artists.

We needed an identity that could span the entirety of Sydney, celebrating a city that means something different to everyone. Something that wasn’t just going to nod to the Harbour Bridge and the Opera House and call it a day. Our solution? ‘Find your Sydney side’. An idea that celebrates each and every side of the real Sydney while reflecting the array of experiences the festival has to offer. ‘Find your Sydney side’ was brought to life through a logo and design system as big, bold and unapologetic as the city itself.
